1. Reading

Anna and Fatimah Talk About the Weekend


Anna and Fatimah are classmates. On Friday, they talk about their weekend plans.


Anna says, “I love visiting new places on weekends. I also like going to the park with my children. What about you, Fatimah?”


Fatimah says, “I love waking up late and relaxing at home. I don’t like going out on Saturdays. I prefer staying inside and playing with my kids.”


Anna asks, “Do you enjoy cooking?” Fatimah answers, “Yes! I enjoy cooking traditional food from my country. I hate cleaning the kitchen, though!”


They both laugh. Anna says, “Have a great weekend!” Fatimah replies, “You too!”
